Some interesting fact about this boring data

It looks more interesting with some graph

In the past 23 years, the negative balance of payments of US increase dramatically (Imports minus Exports)

The negative difference between import and export (total goods and services) of US international trade 2015 increase almost 14 times as 1992 which mean it create a huge gap between import and export.

The goods gap (imports minus exports) actually causes the negative balance of payments of US

In fact, the balance of services is always perform positively in the past 23 years.(also have a stable increasing trend). However, the balance of goods creates big enormous negative gap after cover the positive value from balance of service.

The balance of goods and the balance of services have two outright trends

The balance of goods of US international trade increase negatively from -96897 to -759307.On the other side, the balance of services of US actually increase positively from 57685 to 219552.

The negative balance of payments (percentage change) of US perform weirdly on 2001 and 2008

The percentage change of negative balance of payments of US perform differently in these two special years, 2001 and 2008. According to our research, September 11 attacks (2001) and finance crisis (2008) which play a vital role of these changes.

Services play a more important role in imports rather than exports

The ratio (exports services / exports total(goods plus services ) ) is 13 percent bigger than the ratio (imports services / imports total (goods plus service) in the past 23 years(taking all the average value from 1992 to 2015)
